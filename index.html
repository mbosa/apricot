Investments abroad and/or foreign assets of a financial nature held during 2025.

How it's calculated:

  • Include both Larkan and Klarna shares held during 2025
  • For shares vested before 2025
    • The start date is Jan 1 2025
    • The start value is the total value on Jan 1 2025
  • For shares vested or issued during 2025
    • The start date is the vesting date
    • The start value is the total value at vesting ("FMV at issuance" in the "My holdings" tab)
  • For shares sold during 2025
    • The end date is the date of the sale
    • The end value is the total value at sale ("Sale Price" in the "Sale requests" tab)
  • For shares not sold during 2025
    • The end date is Dec 31 2025
    • The end value is the total value on Dec 31 2025
  • All final values are converted to EUR using the daily exchange rate EUR-SEK or EUR-USD from the Banca d'Italia (link) at vesting, sale, Jan 01, or Dec 31
  • Values are rounded up or down to the nearest integer

    Modalità di arrotondamento

    Gli importi da indicare nella dichiarazione devono essere arrotondati all'unità di euro per eccesso se la frazione decimale è uguale o superiore a cinquanta centesimi di euro oppure per difetto se inferiore a questo limite (ad esempio 65,50 diventa 66; 65,51 diventa 66; 65,49 diventa 65).

  • Shares are aggregated by start and end dates

Notes:

  • October Larkan shares vested on Oct 1 and were sold on Oct 24 at the same value. Due to the daily EUR-SEK exchange rate, their start and end values in EUR are different

Capital gains.

How it's calculated:

  • Include both converted Larkan shares and sold Klarna shares
  • For Larkan shares converted to Klarna
    • Costi: value of the Larkan shares at vesting ("Cost Basis at Issuance" in the "Sale requests" tab)
    • Corrispettivi: value of the Larkan shares at conversion ("Sale Price" in the "Sale requests" tab)
  • For Klarna shares sold during the year
    • Costi: value of the Klarna shares at issuance ("Cost Basis at Issuance" in the "Sale requests" tab)
    • Corrispettivi: value of the Klarna shares at sale ("Sale Price" in the "Sale requests" tab)
  • All costi and corrispettivi are converted to EUR for each individual sale/conversion using the daily exchange rate EUR-SEK or EUR-USD from the Banca d'Italia (link) at vesting, issuance, or sale
  • Total values are rounded up or down to the nearest integer

    Modalità di arrotondamento

    Gli importi da indicare nella dichiarazione devono essere arrotondati all'unità di euro per eccesso se la frazione decimale è uguale o superiore a cinquanta centesimi di euro oppure per difetto se inferiore a questo limite (ad esempio 65,50 diventa 66; 65,51 diventa 66; 65,49 diventa 65).

Notes:

  • Only the totals should be reported
  • Capital gains are taxed at 26% and should be added to the section "Sezione II - Plusvalenze assoggettate ad imposta sostitutiva del 26%"
